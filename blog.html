The Importance of verifying your potential tenants

If you are a landlord directly managing your properties or you use the services of a property manager to oversee the affairs of your properties, you definitely ought to be in the know of your potential tenants. Studies have shown that several landlords who embarked on verifying their potential tenants unravelled that more than 25% of them had some elements of criminal records. This is an important information landlords want to know beforehand.

As a landlord, a thorough verification of your potential tenants prior to moving into your properties is key to avoiding stories that touch the heart. No landlord wants to rent to a tenant that cannot pay his/her rents. No landlord would want to rent to someone that would destroy his property. Basically, no landlord wants a non-paying and problematic tenants.

To bridge this gap, tenant verification is helpful for conducting a thorough background checks, employment history and credit reports on potential tenants. Verification from a potential tenant's previous landlord is also essential. Tenrify conducts background checks on tenants to give a peace of mind to landlords.
