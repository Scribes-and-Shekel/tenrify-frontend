STEPS

Allow us verify your tenants with ease!

Tenant's Invitation

Tenrify sends an email containing an online rental application form to the landlords.

Landlords then issue the online rental application form to their potential tenants.

Tenant's application

Potential tenants then completes the online application form.

This requires providing information about their income source, financial, present employment, employment history and rental history.

Verification

Potential tenants submit their completed application form online.

Tenrify conducts a thorough verification based on the given parameters to ascertain the honesty in the tenant's application.

Landlord gets report

Tenrify generates an insightful report and delivers it to the landlord.

Landlord reviews the report and then makes the right decision on the right tenant to accept.
